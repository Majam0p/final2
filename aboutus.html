About Us

Welcome to the college recipe book! We are all trying to figure out how to pass finals, stay socially relevant, and if we should drop out to join your roommate's rap duo. While you are going through life's tough choices

Founded by starved students, our mission is to bring control back to your diet, or lack there of it. We aim to get you fed in the least amount of time and money possible. Join us in our journey! For a little bit of time tho, this is not a sustainable diet.
